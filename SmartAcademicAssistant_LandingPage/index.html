Smart Academic Assistant

Extract. Summarize. Cite. All in one click — without leaving your research tab.

Problem

Students and researchers waste valuable time copying and pasting abstracts and citations between browser tabs and AI tools. This workflow is slow, distracting, and repetitive. Our extension fixes that.

Solution

The Smart Academic Assistant Chrome extension extracts metadata from academic paper pages like Google Scholar and arXiv, then presents AI-powered actions (summarize, cite, explain) in a clean popup.

Key Features

  • One-click GPT-powered summarization of abstracts.
  • Generate citations instantly in APA or BibTeX formats.
  • Extract structured data (title, authors, abstract, etc.).
  • Built-in academic prompts (e.g., "Explain this method").
  • No tab-switching — everything happens in-page.

Target Users

University students, graduate researchers, academic writers, and research professionals who frequently consume, analyze, and cite academic content.

User Stories

Student: "I want to summarize an abstract so I can decide quickly whether to read the whole paper."
Researcher: "I need citations in APA format for my bibliography."
PhD Candidate: "I want to save my favorite papers for later."
Professor: "I want AI to generate class discussion questions from paper abstracts."
Science Communicator: "I want layperson summaries I can share with a general audience."
